I like wearing bold patterns and many of my favorite inspirational quotes come from children's books. Trained as a fine artist and then a designer, illustration and hand-lettering are often at the core of my brand design. Self-taught as a UI/UX designer, I am always experimenting with new ways to create a memorable in-app experience. You’ll find more of my work here.
